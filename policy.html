Customer Policy

Our policy ensures a positive customer experience.

No Makeup Please remove all makeup prior to services to get the maximum amount of time for your appointment. Please DO NOT curl your natural eyelashes and DO remove all eye makeup with a mild, oil-free cleanser prior to your appointment. Eye makeup can interfere with the adhesive bond and shorten the lifespan of your lash extensions, so itâ€™s very important your natural lashes be as clean as possible. If you are a contact lens wearer, please remove them. Late Policy Should you be running late, we will hold your appointment for 15 minutes provided we hear from you. If you are more than 15 minutes late, we may need to shorten your service, or your appointment may need to be rescheduled in order to remain on time for all our clients. Reservations & Cancellations Extensive and/or multiple salon services may require a major credit card to secure your appointment. If you must reschedule or cancel your appointment(s), please notify us 24 hours in advance to avoid an automatic credit card charge of percentage of the service total.

  • A cancellation fee of 50% applies for cancellations or any changes made less than 24 hours in advance, or in case of no-show.

Allergies We do not offer refunds on allergic reactions. If you are sensitive, or have had a reaction before please let our tech know and we will do a patch test. Phone Policy We ask before you enter our salon that you please turn your phone off, and make any calls or texts before appointments. Child Safety We love children and we hope you understand. For safety and insurance purposes, no child under the age of 12 may accompany you while you are having a service. We do not have the facilities to care for children. Your understanding is greatly appreciated. Lash Fills For booking a lash fill appointment, the price and appointment time are different depending on how often you come back for a fill.
